# Azure AI Foundry & SDK

Azure AI Foundry is the **Unified Platform** for building custom agents.

## 1. Why Foundry?
Foundry consolidates **AI Studio** and **Azure Machine Learning**.

*   **Unified Model Catalog:** ACCESS TO GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, and Llama 4 in one place.
*   **Prompt Flow:** A visual/code-based tool to build agentic logic.
*   **Evaluation:** Tools to measure "Faithfulness," "Relevance," and "Coherence."

## 2. The Agent SDK
This is the core for professional developers building custom agents.

*   **Logic:** Think → Plan → Act.
*   **Tools:** Bind custom Python/JS functions to the agent.
*   **Context:** The SDK handles "Conversation History" and "State Management."

## 3. RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
Foundry makes RAG easy.
*   **Index Builder:** Automatically chunk and embed your SharePoint or PDF data.
*   **Vector Search:** Ground the AI in your private business data.
