## Why this matters

This section is curated for **reliability over hype**. Focus on concepts that repeatedly appear in architecture discussions and exam scenarios.

## Core Concepts

- Define the service boundary clearly.
- Understand capability vs limitation before implementation.
- Apply security, compliance, and observability from day 1.

## Practical Checklist

1. Map business requirement → AI capability.
2. Select managed services before custom ML where possible.
3. Design fallback and human escalation paths.
4. Add logging, cost controls, and quality evaluation.

## Common Mistakes

- Treating copilots as fully autonomous systems.
- Ignoring grounding data quality and permissions.
- Skipping responsible AI controls.
