# Copilot & Agent Admin: Licensing & Usage

The administrative domain accounts for **25-30%** of the AB-900 exam.

## 1. Licensing Models
Microsoft offers two primary licensing models for Copilot.
- **Monthly Subscription:** Fixed cost per user per month.
- **Pay-as-you-go (PAYG):** Usage-based billing (e.g., SharePoint and custom agents).
- **Entra ID:** Licensing is managed through the M365 Admin Center.

## 2. Usage & Adoption
- **Copilot Analytics:** View how many users are active and what features they use.
- **M365 Admin Center:** Go to **Reports > Usage** to see activity by service (Teams, SharePoint, Copilot).

## 3. Custom Agents Lifecycle
- **Creation:** Done in Copilot Studio or SharePoint.
- **Approval:** Admins must approve agents before they are available in the app catalog.
- **Monitoring:** Power Platform Admin Center handles the operational insights.

## 4. Prompt Management
- **Prompt Library:** Saving and sharing prompt templates.
- **Prompt Protection:** Ensuring prompts do not violate DLP or compliance policies.
