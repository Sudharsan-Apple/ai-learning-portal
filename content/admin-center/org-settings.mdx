# M365 Admin Center Essentials

The **Microsoft 365 Admin Center** is the primary portal for managing your organization's users, domains, and settings.

## 1. Domains (The Entry Point)
Every Microsoft 365 tenant comes with an `onmicrosoft.com` domain.
- **Custom Domains:** You can add your own (e.g., `contoso.com`) by adding a **TXT or MX record** to your DNS provider.
- **Primary Domain:** This is the default for new users.

## 2. Org Settings (Global Controls)
Found under **Settings > Org settings**, this controls how features work across the tenant.
- **Services:** Enable/disable features like Microsoft Teams, Planner, and Copilot.
- **Security & Privacy:** Password policies, idle session timeouts, and MFA settings.

## 3. License Management
- **Individual Assignment:** Users > Active users > Licenses and apps.
- **Group-Based Licensing:** Manage via Microsoft Entra ID. Licenses are automatically assigned to anyone who joins a group.
